About me

My name is Alaa Mahrous, I am from Damascus, Syria. I am 28 years old and a graduate of Damascus University, Faculty of Fine Arts, with a major in Graphic Design, achieving a GPA of 78%.

I have been volunteering with UNICEF since 2018 in the field of psychological support for children and encouraging their return to schools, I am a member of the Artists'Syndicate in Damascus. I worked with Al-Mokhtar Trading Group as an advertising designer, creating covers for school books and novels for a period of 5 years, in addition to my freelance work.
